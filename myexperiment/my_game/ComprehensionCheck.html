Game Instructions
  • There are two roles: Buyer and Seller.
  • Each round, the Buyer decides whether to buy a product and how much to pay (any amount from 0 to 100 tokens). The Seller does not make any decisions.
  • The Buyer's payoff is: Endowment - Amount Paid + Product Utility (if bought; otherwise, payoff is 0).
  • The Seller's payoff is: Amount Paid - Production Cost (if bought; otherwise, payoff is 0).
  • Product utility and production cost are randomized each round.
  • Roles may swap in different rounds.
