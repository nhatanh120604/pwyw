Thank you for participating in our study. Your decision data has been securely saved.

This study examines decision-making under a Pay-What-You-Want (PWYW) pricing mechanism, where participants are allowed to choose any amount to pay for a product. The primary research question of this study is “Decision-making in the absence of fixed prices: How does disclosing a suggested price influence customer willingness-to-pay in pay-what-you-want?”. The study was initially presented under a general research title, and the specific PWYW mechanism was not explicitly mentioned beforehand. This was necessary to ensure that participants made decisions without being influenced by prior expectations of the pricing model. Your data will now help us understand the psychological drivers behind voluntary payments.
