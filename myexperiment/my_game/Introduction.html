You are about to participate in a multi-round economic game where you will interact with another anonymous participant to trade a hypothetical product. The game requires you to make purchasing and pricing decisions based on your assigned role and specific pricing conditions.

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the first round.

BUYER ROLE

Role

You have been randomly assigned the role of the BUYER. The other person is in the role of the SELLER.

Endowment

You are endowed with 100 tokens, you can decide how much to pay the SELLER for Product A. You can choose any amount between 0 and 100 tokens.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical product that gives you personal utility (shown to you each round).

Payoff

If you choose to buy the product:

  • The SELLER will receive the amount you choose to pay minus the production cost.
  • You will keep any remaining tokens plus the value you derive from Product A.

If you choose to NOT buy the product:

  • Each of you will receive zero.
At each session, you will be asked:
  • Do you want to buy Product A?
  • If yes, how many tokens out of your 100-token endowment would you like to transfer to the SELLER? You will receive the product at any cost.

SELLER ROLE

Role

You have been randomly assigned the role of the SELLER. The other person is in the role of the BUYER.

Endowment

You are endowed with 0 tokens in this round. You do not make any decision. The BUYER can decide how much of his/her 100 tokens endowment to transfer to you and he/she can choose any amount between 0 and 100 tokens.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical product that costs a certain amount (shown to you each round) to produce.

Payoff

If the BUYER chooses to buy the product:

  • You receive the amount the BUYER decides to transfer to you minus the production cost.
  • The BUYER receives the amount he/she decides not to transfer and thus to keep.

If the BUYER chooses to NOT buy the product:

  • Each of you will receive zero.

Payment Information

In each round, you will earn an income based on your role and decisions. Your income is calculated separately for each round and will be shown to you at the end of that round.

Income BUYER SELLER
Decides to buy the product Remaining endowment - Price paid + Utility Price paid - Production cost
Decides not to buy the product 0 0

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected by the computer. Only the income from the selected round will be used to determine your final monetary payment. All other rounds are for experimental purposes only. The selection is fair and transparent, which means every round has an equal chance of being chosen.

Conversion rate: 100 tokens = 50,000 VND. Your final payment equals the income from the selected round converted to cash.
