You are about to participate in a multi-round economic game where you will interact with another anonymous participant to trade a hypothetical product. The game requires you to make purchasing and pricing decisions based on your assigned role and specific pricing conditions. Your take-home earnings will be based on your performance in the game.

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the first round.

BUYER ROLE

You have been randomly assigned the role of the BUYER. The other person is in the role of the SELLER.

Endowment

You begin this round with an endowment of 100 tokens. Your task is to decide how much to pay the SELLER for Product A. You may choose to pay any amount between 0 and 100 tokens.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical good. Its utility value will be displayed on your screen at the beginning of each round.

Payoff

If you decide to purchase the product, you will receive it regardless of the price you pay:

  • The SELLER receives the amount you choose to pay, minus the production cost.
  • You receive any remaining endowment plus the utility value you derive from Product A.

If you decide NOT to purchase the product:

  • The SELLER receives 0.
  • You keep the endowment.
Decision Task

In each round, you will be asked to answer the following:

  • Do you want to purchase Product A?
  • If yes, how many tokens from your 100-token endowment would you like to transfer to the SELLER? (Note: You will receive the product regardless of the price you pay)

SELLER ROLE

You have been randomly assigned the role of the SELLER. The other person is in the role of the BUYER.

Endowment

You begin this round with 0 tokens. You do not make any decisions. The BUYER will determine how much of their 100-token endowment to transfer to you for Product A.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical good. The cost to produce this good will be displayed on your screen at the beginning of each round.

Payoff

If the BUYER decides to purchase the product, they will receive it regardless of the price paid:

  • You receive the amount the BUYER chooses to pay, minus the production cost.
  • The BUYER receives their remaining endowment plus the utility value they derive from Product A.

If the BUYER decides NOT to purchase the product:

  • You receive 0.
  • The BUYER keeps the endowment.

Payment Information

In each round, you will earn a payoff based on your role and decisions. Your payoff is calculated separately for each round and will be shown to you at the end of that round.

Payoff BUYER SELLER
Decides to buy the product Endowment - Price paid + Utility value Price paid - Production cost
Decides not to buy the product Endowment 0

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected by the computer. Only the payoff from the selected round will be used to determine your final monetary payment. All other rounds are for experimental purposes only. The selection is fair and transparent, which means every round has an equal chance of being chosen.

Tokens are then converted to VND at a rate of 1 token = 200 VND. In addition to your earnings, you will receive a 20,000 VND show-up fee. If your payoff in the selected round is negative, the loss will be deducted from your show-up fee to determine your final payment.
