You huhuuuh are about to participate in a multi-round economic game where you will interact with another anonymous participant to trade a hypothetical product. The game requires you to make purchasing and pricing decisions based on your assigned role and specific pricing conditions.

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the first round.

BUYER ROLE

Endowment

You begin this round with an endowment of 100 tokens. Your task is to decide how much you wish to pay the SELLER for Product A. You may choose to transfer any amount between 0 and 100 tokens.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical good. Its value to you (your personal utility) will be displayed on your screen at the beginning of each round.

Payoff

If you decide to purchase the product, you will receive it regardless of the price you pay:

  • The SELLER will receive the amount you choose to pay, minus the production cost.
  • You will receive your remaining endowment plus the utility value you derive from Product A.

If you decide NOT to purchase the product:

  • The SELLER will receive 0.
  • You will retain your initial endowment.
Decision Task

In each round, you will be asked to answer the following:

  • Do you wish to purchase Product A?
  • If yes, how many tokens from your 100-token endowment would you like to transfer to the SELLER? (Note: You will receive the product regardless of the amount transferred.)

SELLER ROLE

Endowment

You begin this round with 0 tokens. You do not make any active decisions. The BUYER will determine how much of their 100-token endowment to transfer to you for Product A.

Product

Product A is a hypothetical good. The cost to produce this good will be displayed on your screen at the beginning of each round.

Payoff

If the BUYER decides to purchase the product, they will receive it regardless of the price paid:

  • You will receive the amount the BUYER chooses to pay, minus the production cost.
  • The BUYER will receive their remaining endowment plus the utility value they derive from Product A.

If the BUYER decides NOT to purchase the product:

  • You will receive 0.
  • The BUYER will retain their initial endowment.

Payment Information

In each round, you will earn a payoff based on your role and decisions. Your payoff is calculated separately for each round and will be shown to you at the end of that round.

Payoff BUYER SELLER
Decides to buy the product Endowment - Price paid + Utility value Price paid - Production cost
Decides not to buy the product Endowment 0

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected by the computer. Only the income from the selected round will be used to determine your final monetary payment. All other rounds are for experimental purposes only. The selection is fair and transparent, which means every round has an equal chance of being chosen.

Your final payment equals the token earnings from this selected round plus the token show-up fee. The total token amount is then converted to VND at a predetermined exchange rate. If you have a negative payoff in the selected round, the loss is deducted from your show-up fee tokens before conversion.
